1. CLOUD & SECURITY (The Sync Engine)
============================================================================
- [FREE V1] Buddy Cloud Sync
    Opt-in encrypted vault sync across devices
    Requires a user-held recovery key on each new device
    Not gated by Stripe Premium
    
- [PLANNED] Auto-Backup History
    Versioned data snapshots
    Restore from 7 days, 30 days, or 1 year ago
    
- [PLANNED] Multi-Device Login
    Secure auth (Email/Pass or Passkey)
    Link multiple devices to one account
    
- [PLANNED] Offline-First Conflict Resolution
    Handle simultaneous edits from two offline devices
    Smart merge queue
